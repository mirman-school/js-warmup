Handling Arrays

The life of a coder

It's a reality of code: you're going to work with a lot of arrays. Looping over them, searching through them, modifying them, exporting them. It's the life of a coder.

Look around at how many arrays, collections of things, are around you right now. Bookshelves, keys on a keyboard, silverware, spokes on a bicycle wheel, leaves on a tree. The world is made of arrays. Our information especially.
